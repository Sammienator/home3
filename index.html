FIRM RESUME

More than five years ago, Neema Nyambura, and a few other advocates patnered to establish what is now NEEMA AND CO. Advocates. Samuel Mugo initially a local businessman at the time funded the opening of the firm. The firm is comprised of an aggressive group of trial lawyers with complementary expertise and advocacy skills who have developed a reputation for trial expertise, initiative, responsiveness and hard work, and we strive to provide legal representation for businesses and individuals that is unsurpassed.

Professional Profile

Results-driven corporate lawyer with 3+ years of experience ewithin the Law Sector. Adept at drafting and reviewing policies. Possess a bachelors degree from Daystaruniversity, followed by a brief stint at Kenya Law School, as well as being member of the Republic of Kenya Advocates Bar in good standing. Achieved advantageous settlements in 90% of cases.

We also offer other services including:

  • Securities and Investment Banking
  • Business and Shareholder Disputes
  • Financial Lines, Management and Professional Liability Defense
  • Officer/Director Liability
  • Insurance Coverage Opinions and Declaratory Judgment Litigation
  • Defense of First-party Insurance Claims and Third-party Claims
  • Employment Practices Liability (EPL)
  • Major Personal Injury and Product Liability
  • Life Insurance Disputes
