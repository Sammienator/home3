FIRM RESUME

More than twenty-five years ago, Neema Nyambura, Ronald J. Wasili, Andrew M. Muchangi and Samuel Mugo opened the firm, having previously practiced together at a large multi-city firm for seven years. The firm is comprised of an aggressive group of trial lawyers with complementary expertise and advocacy skills who have developed a reputation for trial expertise, initiative, responsiveness and hard work, and we strive to provide legal representation for businesses and individuals that is unsurpassed.

Professional Profile

Results-driven corporate lawyer with 10+ years of experience ensuring the legality of commercial transactions. Adept at drafting and reviewing policies. Possess a J.D. from Duke Law School and am a member of the New York State Bar in good standing. Achieved advantageous settlements in 90% of cases.

Collectively, our partners bring more than 180 years of trial experience to the firm, having tried to a jury or judge over 250 lawsuits, injunctions and arbitration proceedings covering a wide variety of areas, including

Securities and Investment Banking
Business and Shareholder Disputes
Financial Lines, Management and Professional Liability Defense
Officer/Director Liability
Insurance Coverage Opinions and Declaratory Judgment Litigation
Defense of First-party Insurance Claims and Third-party Claims
Employment Practices Liability (EPL)
Life Insurance Disputes
Major Personal Injury and Product Liability
Probate Litigation

Our lawyers are all actively engaged in trying cases in federal and state courts and various arbitration tribunals. The firm also is dedicated to working with its clients to provide the finest results available in a cost effective manner and to assist the client in addressing increasing concerns with rising legal costs. To this end, and to improve our efficiency, the firm explores alternative fee arrangements on a case-by-case basis and provides each professional with the technology to allow for case management and budgeting, scheduling, deposition and document organization, and data retrieval.
