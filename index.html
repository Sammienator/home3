Immigration Law

Immigration law refers to national government policies controlling the immigration and deportation of people, and other matters such as citizenship. Immigration law varies from country to country and can be the subject of very high profile international cases relating to various topics such as the legality of detention of immigrants and the legal status of aliens without a nationality.
